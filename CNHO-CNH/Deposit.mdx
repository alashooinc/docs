### üõ†Ô∏è **Step-by-Step: Deposit CNHO**

1. Log in to your CNHO account at https://beta.cnho.io/
2. **Click the ‚ÄúDeposit‚Äù button**
3. Select the **Receiving Blockchain** where you want to deposit CNHO
4. Copy the **Deposit Address** shown for the selected blockchain
