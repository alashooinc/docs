To start trading on [**trade.cnho.io**](http://trade.cnho.io), users must deposit supported assets (e.g., CNHO, mBTC, ETH) into their trading account. This guide outlines the process.

---

### Step 1: Log In

- Visit [**trade.cnho.io**](http://trade.cnho.io)
- Log in using your **email and password**
- Ensure your account has passed both **KYC** and **Bank Verification**

---

### Step 2: Navigate to the Asset Details Page

- On the dashboard, locate the asset you wish to deposit (e.g., **CNHO**, **mBTC**, **ETH**)
- Click on the asset to view its **Asset Details Page**

---

### Step 3: Click the â€œDepositâ€ Button

- On the Asset Details Page, click the **â€œDepositâ€** button
- This opens the deposit form for the selected asset

---

### Step 4: Fill in Deposit Information

Complete the deposit form using the information below.

| Field                    | Description                                                                                                     |
| :----------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Receiving Blockchain** | Select the blockchain network where you want to deposit CNHO tokens from (e.g., Ethereum, Solana, Cosmos, etc.) |
| **Deposit Address**      | The CNHO deposit address on the selected blockchain. Send your CNHO tokens to this address.                     |
| **Your Address**         | Your wallet address from which you will send CNHO tokens                                                        |
| **Amount You Deposit**   | Enter the amount of CNHO tokens you want to deposit                                                             |
| _(Your Balance: 0)_      | This displays your current balance of the selected asset in your trading account                                |
